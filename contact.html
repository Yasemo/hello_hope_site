Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book a program?

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance to ensure availability. However, we understand that sometimes urgent needs arise, so please don't hesitate to contact us even for shorter notice requests.

Do you offer virtual programs?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual program delivery options. Our virtual programs are designed to be just as engaging and effective as our in-person sessions.

What is the typical duration of your programs?

Program duration varies depending on your needs. We offer everything from single 90-minute workshops to multi-day intensive training programs. We'll work with you to design something that fits your schedule and objectives.

Do you provide materials and resources?

Yes, all participants receive comprehensive materials including handouts, resources, and follow-up materials to support ongoing implementation of the concepts learned.
