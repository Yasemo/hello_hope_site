Keynote Speakers

Aubrey Noronha

Founder, Hello Hope Canada

"Building Resilient Communities: The Power of Hope-Centered Education"

Drawing from 16+ years of experience in character education and crisis management, Aubrey will share practical strategies for creating safer, more connected communities.

Dr. Sarah Chen

Director of Youth Mental Health, University of Toronto

"Trauma-Informed Approaches to Student Support"

Learn evidence-based strategies for supporting students who have experienced trauma and building healing-centered school environments.

Marcus Williams

Indigenous Education Specialist

"Indigenous Wisdom in Character Education"

Explore how traditional Indigenous teachings can inform modern approaches to character development and community building.
