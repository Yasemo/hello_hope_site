Programs

Character-building programs designed for lasting change

HELLO HOPE

Social media promised connection, but delivered toxic comparison. Now AI is replacing friendships. This session delivers practical tools for building real human bonds that actually stick.

Duration: 60 min | Versions: Gr. 4-12, Faculty, Parents/Guardians, Corporate Teams

Delivered to 10,000+ Participants

COLOUR BLIND?

Good intentions don't fix systemic racism. Silence isn't neutrality. Part 1.0 explores why this fight is a matter of the heart. Part 2.0 delivers actionable steps to be the opposite of racist.

Duration: 60 min each | Versions: Gr. 7-12, Faculty, Sr. Admin., Parents/Guardians, Corporate Teams

Delivered to 8,000+ Participants

STICK WITH THE TRUTH

Our youngest students deserve truth about skin color, the lies they'll hear, and tools to fight back. Not diluted. Not sanitized. Age-appropriate truth-telling that 8-year-olds can handle

Duration: 60 min each (1.0 + 2.0) | Versions: Gr. K-6

Delivered to 5,000+ Participants

I'M JUST HERE

Some kids are existing, not living. Purpose feels like a luxury when you're just surviving. Raw talk on self-identity, peer conflicts, and the secret to supreme satisfaction.

Duration: 60 min | Versions: Gr. 7-12

Delivered to 7,000+ Participants

MAN UP

Boys are getting masculinity lessons from influencers. This session delivers straight conversation about male influence on culture and practical tools for using it for good.

Duration: 60 min | Versions: Gr. 6-12, Teams

Delivered to 6,000+ Participants

RECONNECT

Teachers are exhausted. Administrators are overwhelmed. Everyone's running on empty. This one-hour recharge helps your team reconnect to what matters and reach the finish line.

Duration: 60 min | Versions: Educators, Administration, Special Teams

Delivered to 3,000+ Participants
